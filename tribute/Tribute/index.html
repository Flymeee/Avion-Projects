The Batman

"Darkness isn't his enemy. It's his ally."


Batman is one of the most iconic superheroes in comic book history, created by artist Bob Kane and writer Bill Finger.
He made his first appearance in Detective Comics #27 in 1939. Unlike many other superheroes, Batman does not possess superhuman abilities.
Instead, he relies on his intelligence, physical prowess, and vast wealth to fight crime.
